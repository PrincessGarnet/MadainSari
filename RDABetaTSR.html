THE SPONSORED RIDE

in aid of Cranleigh Riding for the Disabled Association Group
11 mile ride on Albury Heath & Farley Heath, South of Guildford also
3 mile route for led or acompanied riders.

Good Friday
14th April 2017

-Two start points-
Albury Heath & Farley Heath

11 mile ride

The ride takes place over beautiful heath and through woodland south of Guildford. The going is mainly on sandy paths and bridleways with occasional stony tracks. There are 2 starting points (see venue map)

  • The cricket ground, Albury Heath GU5 9DB

  • Roman Temple, Farley Heath

    • A refreshment van and loos will be on site at Albury Heath. There is no entry fee but there is a minimum sponsorship of £35.

3 mile ride

This ride is for reiders who are lef or acompanied by a responsible person ON FOOT ONLY. It is suitable for young or inexperiences riders, perhaps taking part in a sponsored ride for the first time, and takes about 60-75 minutes to complete. The ride follows mainly sandy paths and a quiet single track lane (no through road). However, some parts of the route can be muddy, so thosepeople who are accompanying riders on foot are recommended to wear wellington boots or similar footwear. Well behaved dogs are permitted on leads only. There is no entry fee but there is a minimum sponsorship of £25.

Sponsorship

Minimum sponsorship is £35 for the 11mile and £25 for the 3 mile ride, but please raise as much as you can! Please GiftAid your donations as this raises Cranleigh RDA Group an extra 25p forevery £1 you give. All proceeds go towards the upkeep of our ponies and the facilities at Casi’s Farm, Cranleigh. Additional Sponsorship forms are available on the website. Use our MyDonate site to create your own Fundraising Page. Simply go to My Donate and follow the link Start Fundraising Entries Download your Entry Form from our website or phone Jan Butcher on 01403 753577


We are offering a prize of £50 to the adult aged 18 or over, and to the person aged under 18 on the day of ride who raises the most sponsoship money in their age category so ask your family, friends and colleagues to help you raise as much as possible.

All sponsoship money recieved by 15th May 2017 will be counted towards your total. Prize winners will be notified as soon as possible thereafter.

  • Entries close on Wednesday 12th April 2017. We cannot accept entries on the day but do telephone Jan Butcher on 01403 753577 up to the day before to check if a late entry can be accepted.
  • Please note that we limit numbers taking part out of respect for other users of the heath and because of limited parking.
  • To avoid congestion, start times will be at intervals from 9.30am. Please do not turn up hoping to ride if you haven’t been given a start time.

Rosettes on Completion of the Ride

Please return your numbered bib to the Secretary's tent at the end of both the 11 mile and 3 mile rides to confirm your safe return to the marshals and to collect your rosette.

Photographers will be on site

Official Photography will be provided by MH Photos and photos can be purchased from their website. 10% Of proceeds will be donated to Cranleigh RDA Group.

Thank you from all the riders, staff, volunteers and ponies at Cranleigh RDA Group!
