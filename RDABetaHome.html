RDA Cranleigh

Cranleigh RDA Group is a charity that provides riding opportunities to over 200 adults and children, delivering both physical benefits and psychological wellbeing Casi's farm is a dedicated RDA centre, with nine ponies, two arenas, stabling and paddocks, all run by a stable manager and 190 volunteers.

Fund Raising

Annual running costs of £50,000 are primarily met through donations, fundraising activites and contributions from our riders. 100% of the money you raise will go towards the day to day running costs of the Group
