Cranleigh Riding for the Disabled Association Group

All proceeds from this ride go to the Cranleigh RDA Group which, apart from one full time equivalent paid member of staff, relies on over 180 dedicated volunteers to deliver its service.
The Group is based at Casi’s Farm, Cranleigh and provides riding for over 200 riders with special needs. The Group depends heavily on fundraising and other donations to run the organisation. Every penny you raise on this sponsored ride will be going towards the upkeep of the facilities and the health and comfort of the ponies so that they can continue doing this worthwhile job. We are always looking for volunteers. You don’t have to be experienced with horses or people with special needs, just have enthusiasm and a willingness to learn and have at least two hours to spare each week. The work is very rewarding and fulfilling to everyone, riders and helpers alike.
For more information, visit our website or phone our Chair, Liz Harrison on 01483 222429

Thank you from all the riders, staff, volunteers and ponies at Cranleigh RDA Group!
